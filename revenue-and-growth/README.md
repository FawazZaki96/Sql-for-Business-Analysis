# Revenue & Growth Analysis (SQL)

## Business Questions
- How much revenue are we generating?
- Are we growing month-over-month?
- What is the cumulative revenue trend?
- Which periods show acceleration or slowdown?

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Monthly Revenue
- Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth %
- Cumulative Revenue (Running Total)
- Year-to-Date (YTD) Performance

## Techniques
- GROUP BY with time dimensions
- Window Functions (LAG, SUM OVER)
- Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
