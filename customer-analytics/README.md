# Customer Analytics (SQL)

## Business Questions
- How many customers are new vs returning each month?
- What is the Average Order Value (AOV) by customer segment?
- Do the top 20% of customers generate ~80% of revenue (Pareto)?
- What is a proxy for Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)?

## KPIs
- New vs Returning Customers
- AOV by Segment
- Top Customers Contribution (80/20)
- Customer Revenue & Order Frequency

## Techniques
- CTEs
- Window Functions (LAG, RANK, SUM OVER)
- Conditional Aggregation
- Percentile / Cumulative Share
